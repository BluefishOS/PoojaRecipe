Litti Chokha

  • Prepration & Cooking time
  • Ingredients
  • Cooking method
  • Tips & Tricks

Prepration Time 15-20 minutes

Cooking time 40-45 minutes

Serving 2-3 person

For Dough
  • 1 Cup Wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water for kneading
For Stuffing
  • 1/2 Cup roasted gram flour/sattu
  • 1 medium size chopped onion
  • 1-2 chopped green chilli
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
For Stuffing
  • 4 medium size potatoes
  • 3 medium size eggplants
  • 2 medium size tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 chopped green chilli
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
Garnish
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  • 1/2 cup Ghee
