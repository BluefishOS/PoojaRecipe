Litti Chokha

  • Prepration & Cooking time
  • Ingredients
  • Cooking method
  • Tips & Tricks

Prepration Time 15-20 minutes

Cooking time 40-45 minutes

Serving 2-3 person

For Dough
  • 1 Cup Wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water for kneading
For Stuffing
  • 1/2 Cup roasted gram flour/sattu
  • 1 medium size chopped onion
  • 1-2 chopped green chilli
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp carom seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
For Stuffing
  • 4 medium size potatoes
  • 3 medium size eggplants
  • 2 medium size tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 chopped green chilli
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
Garnish
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  • 1/2 cup Ghee

Hummus

  • Prepration & Cooking time
  • Ingredients
  • Cooking method
  • Tips & Tricks

Prepration Time 10 minutes

Cooking time for chickpeas 15 minutes

Serving 2,3 People

  • Cooked Chickpeas- 11/2Cup
  • Green Chilli - 2
  • Tahini- 2 tbsp
  • Garlic Gloves -2 pod
  • Lemon Juice -2 tbsp
  • Salt -as per your taste
  • Water or aquafaba - 2 to 3 tbspn
  • Olive oil- 1 tbspn

  • Take a mixture grinder jar put cooked chickpeas and add water or aquafaba and blend it until creamy texture.
  • Add Oilve oil, lemon juice,tahini, garlic pod, chilli, Salt and blend for 30 sec.
  • Serve hummus with a drizzle of olive oil.

  • If you will remove chickpeas cover you will get better taste.
  • For flavour you can add roasted beetroot and bell pepper.
  • Serve it with crackers, tortilla wrap and flat bread also.
  • Store it and airtight container and refrigerate upto one week.
